What’s the Point of this Blog?

2019-06-09 — Written by Dillon de Silva
#blog  #introduction 

Where do I start?

Have you ever sat in front of a computer with the intention of doing something productive? Your mind focuses as you sit in front of the glowing screen where magic is possible. You relax, take a sip of your coffee, place your hands on the keyboard and… nothing

This is me right now. A clueless 15 year old who’s not quite sure why he’s starting a blog just yet. Sure, I definitely had the intention of sharing what I’m doing as well as my perspectives on a multitude of topics but I still haven’t figured out why I’m REALLY starting a blog. Why I’m planning on putting hours of work into writing articles which aren’t going to change the fact that Donald Trump is the President of America or fix extreme poverty in South Africa.

Maybe in time, I’ll figure out what motive force drove me to write this article and start a blog on this day. Perhaps, I’ll even blog about it when I figure it out. Until then, here’s a little bit about me and what you can expect me to write about.

My Hobbies

“My hobbies just sort of became my vocation” - Al Yankovic

I think without hobbies, there would be some serious colour drainage from our lives. Why so? Because hobbies define who we are. What we like. How we choose to spend our time. Our hobbies become our passions and for many of us (like ‘Weird Al’), they become our careers.

I’m still relatively young (although occasionally, I like to think I’m an old, dopey, know it all jackass) so my hobbies haven’t become a career just yet. In fact, I’m not too sure what career I’d like to be in when I grow up. But, my hobbies are a vital part of my personality composition.

Without further ado, I’m into:

  • Building stuff (I’m in love with STEM)
  • Music (I play guitar and piano)
  • Photography
  • Socialising with friends and family (I mean who isn’t?)
  • Fitness (I play soccer and run long distances)
  • Coffee and Chocolate (Mocha, mocha, mocha)

What I’ll Write About?

This is the part of the blog which sets the agenda. So in a way, I have to not screw this up.

And in order to not screw up, I need to write about one simple thing…

Anything!

I’ll write about what I’m up to. What I’ve learnt. Problems I’ve faced. Problems my mates have faced. You could essentially say, I’m going to build up a digital archive of life experiences.

And I want them to help you. To inspire you. To make you wake up and think, “I can do anything”. If I can do that, then maybe… just maybe…

I would’ve fulfilled this blog’s purpose.

Because you see, life is like a deck of cards. You draw some into your hand and have to play them accordingly to win. And right now, I’m playing the ace of blogs card down.

I’m not too sure where that analogy just came from but let’s roll with it.

I’m not allowed to drink alcoholic beverages just yet (legal age in Australia is 18), so I invite you to have a virtual glass of choccy milk with me as we toast to the conclusion of this article and the beginning of a new chapter in the history of this blog.
