A Momentary Appreciation for Sound

2020-04-08 — Written by Dillon de Silva
#poetry 

If all the sounds in the world vanished amongst silence
If the unique spectrum of tones were to diffuse away
Oh the treachery
For I cannot imagine such a world
A world without sound

Like the waves radiating through the oceans
She travels far and wide
The wild summer parties manifesting with adolescent romance
The old couple sharing a slow dance
Whilst the young ones learn to boogie

Yet here I lay
Raw and exposed within my despair
Stuck in a dilemma
Hands cupped to my ears
Wondering how I blocked sound

I loved sound
I miss sound
For she was my peace in the heart of a war
More delicate than the glass she can break
I do not deserve such stunning yet intricate complexion

Therefore when she returns
And she will
I will not be oblivious to the love she protrudes
The tantalising sensation within my heart
A motivational kick to the ass

Because sound is of a certain class
A vibration that can tranquilise a bear in the wild
That echoes within the spirits of free dolphins
She loves me and now more than ever
I love her
