Gifts

2020-04-14
#poetry 

The beauty that resides within nature
Is one of which provides a bird with wings
A fish with gills
And mechanics to a beaver

Though you and I alike
May not possess such roaring crafts
The light whose hand at which we were bred
Gifted each and everyone of us
With a power unlike no other

The neurons firing within the complexity of the mind
Or the reverberation of our heart with song
Perhaps you wield a pencil as your weapon
Writing away to enlighten the souls of others
As I strive to do so now

Yet on a deeper more profound level
You and I are capable of so much more
I have seen forces
To which the cocoon surrenders
Birthing a perfectly symmetrical flutter on this planet

I have seen benignity
Affirmed within others
Like the jigsaw puzzles
Completed by sweet, sweet children
On a blissful Sunday afternoon

I have seen a mythical modesty
Achieved by focused beings
Their eyes shut
To the tremendous gold that lies before them
So as to continue a quest beyond the stars

I have seen the arms of a thrower
Endurance within the legs of a runner
And mentality of a fighter
Exhaling enormous ability
To overcome an adversary by the name of pain

These are not the only traits
Of which one could possess
Rather an idea
Exemplifying the power
Of gifts bestowed upon us

In a search greater than the Himalayas
As you climb to the top
Discover your powers
For they are there
Unique to all of us

But do not be so ignorant
To not use your powers
Use them with care
With love
Against the shed of blood

Because I know
If we were to all lay our guns flat
And use our gifts for the purpose of aiding one another
Earth would forever be the radiator
Of eternal love and peace through time and space
