A Little Update on Me

2020-03-27

Well Firstly…

Needless to say, I haven’t blogged about anything for quite a significant period of time. However, that’s not to say that life has been uneventful.

Truth is the last six months have been rejuvenating for me.

  • I travelled overseas to America with my family.

  • I was provided with the incredible opportunity to return to the National Computer Science School.

  • I deleted social media spontaneously for an extended period of time.

  • I started my senior years of high school.

The circumstances have changed around me but so have I. I have experienced the greatest feelings of anxiety concerning a world after high school. I have felt emotions of love and confusion in a new light. I have stretched my mind to new lengths in an attempt to learn new things. I have listened to and played more music than any other point in my life. I have met some truly inspirational people who have changed my life.

I have also experienced the thrill of the Incredicoaster in Disneyland (three times to be precise).

It came to my attention sometime last week that I needed to write. The bubble was bursting and so after six months ish of not blogging, I’m back in front of my laptop writing this sentence.

I hope to be more consistent on here this year which is what this post is about. It’s a little reminder to myself to keep writing…

Because in a way I missed this little hobby of mine. What once started out as a little experiment has somehow paved its way as something I want to have as an important part of my life. Now more than ever, I feel a greater need to blog. Blogs provide a passageway for expression. A platform for conversation and reflection. Being away from it for so long has only improved my willingness to keep this up!
